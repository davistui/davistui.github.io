## My Approach to Enterprise Sales

> "The hard question doesn't kill the deal; it uncovers the truth."

My sales philosophy is built on technical execution, rigorous qualification, and deep economic alignment. Over 10 years in SaaS and enterprise sales, I've learned that complex deals aren't won by pitching features—they are won by navigating internal variance and solving friction points across people and process.

*   **Rigorous Qualification:** I don't chase smoke. I focus on identifying real economic buyers, uncovering true compelling events, and understanding the cost of inaction early in the cycle.
*   **Technical & Data-Driven Execution:** Enterprise software requires more than relationship building. I dive deep into technical architecture, integration landscapes, and data flows to ensure alignment with engineering and product leaders.
*   **Mutual Value Alignment:** A successful cycle is a collaborative project. I build transparent, mutual evaluation plans with champions to ensure both sides have skin in the game from discovery to close.
